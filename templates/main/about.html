Our Story

Serving the Greater St. Louis Sikh Community Since 1986

The Sikh Study Circle of St. Louis was established in 1986 as a spiritual and cultural center for the growing Sikh community in the St. Louis metropolitan area. Located in the heart of St. Peters, Missouri, our Gurdwara has been a beacon of Sikh values, traditions, and community service for over three decades.

What began as a small gathering of dedicated Sikh families has grown into a vibrant community center that serves hundreds of families across the greater St. Louis region. Our location at 116 Willis Dr, Saint Peters, has become a cornerstone for spiritual guidance, cultural preservation, and community outreach.
